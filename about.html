About
 

Coffee is always a welcome topic of conversation. Check out:

  • J. Hoffmann's or L. Hedrick Youtube channels (if you are curious about specialty coffee),

  • thecoffeepodcast (if you are curious about the history of coffee), and

  • Scott Rao's The Coffee Roasters Companion (if you want to get into roasting coffee).

A brief list of my favourite cafes and roasteries includes Yuán Coffee, Nemesis Coffee, Bugan Coffee Lab, MAME, Revolver, Rogue Roasters, Cafe Giang, Single O, Gardelli Specialty Coffees, Drip Roasters, intelligentsia, Aparment Coffee.

I love modern art, puns and memes. A few pointers: xkcd, perry bible, joan cornellá, ruthmulvie.com, willyramos.com, randomgallery.it.

For LaTex and Math aficionados: texample, fermatslibrary, genealogy.math, mathcounterexamples.

I have a strong passion for mountain activities and outdoor adventures.
