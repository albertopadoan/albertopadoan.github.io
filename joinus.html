About the Lab

The research group is led by Dr. Alberto Padoan.

We are hosted by the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of British Columbia on Point Grey campus, located on the unceded lands of the xʷməθkʷəy̓əm (Musqueam) people.

Nestled between forests, mountains, and the ocean, Vancouver is consistently celebrated as one of the most diverse and livable cities worldwide.

The lab offers a vibrant and inclusive environment to develop expertise in control, optimization, and computing while addressing meaningful engineering challenges.

Join us

Minimum Qualifications

  • A Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in mathematics, physics, engineering, or a related field.

  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills.

  • Completion of an introductory course in control systems.

  • Above all, a passion for learning and a growth-oriented mindset.

How to apply: Email the following documents to apadoan {at} ethz.ch.

  • Your resume/CV.

  • A cover letter highlighting relevant experience and research interests.

  • Transcripts of records.

Application Guidelines:

  • Combine all documents into a single .pdf file.

  • Use the subject line: “[UBC Application]: LastName, FirstName”.

Important Notes:

  • Applications that do not follow submission guidelines will _not_ be considered.

  • Given the large number of speculative emails, only shortlisted candidates may be contacted for an online interview.

Diversity and Inclusion: I strongly encourage applications from individuals belonging to historically underrepresented and marginalized groups, including Indigenous peoples, racialized persons, women, persons with disabilities, and 2SLGBTQ+ individuals.
