Join the Padoan Lab at UBC

Qualifications

  • A Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in mathematics, physics, engineering, or a related field.

  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills.

  • Completion of an introductory course in control systems.

  • Above all, a passion for learning and a growth-oriented mindset.

How to apply
Email the following documents to:

alberto [DOT] padoan [@] ubc [DOT] ca

  • Your resume/CV.

  • A cover letter highlighting relevant experience and research interests.

  • Transcripts of records.

Guidelines

  • Combine all documents into a single .pdf file.

  • Use the subject line: “[UBC Application]: LastName, FirstName”.

Notes

  • Applications that do not follow submission guidelines will not be considered.

  • Given the large number of speculative emails, only shortlisted candidates may be contacted for an online interview.
