About the Lab

We are hosted by the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of British Columbia on Point Grey campus, located on the unceded lands of the xʷməθkʷəy̓əm (Musqueam) people.

Nestled between forests, mountains, and the ocean, Vancouver is consistently celebrated as one of the most diverse and livable cities worldwide.

The lab offers a vibrant and inclusive environment to develop expertise in control, optimization, and computing while addressing meaningful engineering challenges.

We are not currently accepting new applications.
