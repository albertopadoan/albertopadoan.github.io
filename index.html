Research interests

Dynamical systems and control theory, with special emphasis on modelling, approximation and robustness of nonlinear systems.

About me

I received the Laurea Magistrale degree (cum laude) in Ingegneria dell'Automazione from the University of Padova in 2013 and the Ph.D. degree in Control Theory from the Control and Power Group at Imperial College London in 2017. I then joined the Control Group and Sidney Sussex College as a Research Associate at the University of Cambridge.
