Assistant Professor, Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering, University of British Columbia.

Creator and host of the inControl podcast.

Research interests

Control theory. Modeling, analysis, and design of dynamical systems, with applications to biological and cyber-physical systems.

About me

I received the Laurea Magistrale degree (cum laude) in Ingegneria dell'Automazione from the University of Padova in 2013 and the Ph.D. degree in Control Theory from the Control and Power Group at Imperial College London in 2017, under the supervision of Alessandro Astolfi. In 2018, I then joined the Control Group and Sidney Sussex College as a Research Associate at the University of Cambridge to collaborate with Rodolphe Sepulchre. In 2021, I became a member of the Automatic Control Laboratory at ETH Zürich and of NCCR Automation, working primarily with Florian Dörfler and John Lygeros.
